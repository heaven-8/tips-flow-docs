## Wetin be crypto-tips?

**crypto-tips** dey let customers leave tip for **USDC** (digital dollar) when dem scan QR code.

Staff go register for app come choose one role — **Manager** or **Waiter**. Customers wey dey pay tip **no need account**; dem just scan come pay from their own wallet or exchange app.

## How e dey work

1. **Manager** go create venue and waiter profiles with QR codes.
2. **Waiters** go claim their profile through invite link (or register by themselves).
3. Customers scan QR code come pay with USDC.
4. Waiters check balance come withdraw to their own wallet when dem ready.
