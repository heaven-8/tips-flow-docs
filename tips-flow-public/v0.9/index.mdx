## What is crypto-tips?

**crypto-tips** lets customers leave tips in **USDC** (digital dollars) by scanning a QR code.

Staff sign up in the app and choose a role — **Manager** or **Waiter**. Customers who pay tips do **not** need an account; they only scan and pay from their own wallet or exchange app.

## How it works

1. A **Manager** creates the venue and waiter profiles with QR codes.
2. **Waiters** claim their profile via an invite link (or sign up on their own).
3. Customers scan the QR code and pay in USDC.
4. Waiters check their balance and withdraw to their own wallet when ready.
