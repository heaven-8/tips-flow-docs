## Overview

**crypto-tips** lets customers leave tips in **USDC** (digital dollars) by scanning a QR code.

Staff sign up in the app and pick one of two roles: **Manager** or **Waiter**.

Customers who pay tips do **not** need an account — they only scan and pay from their own wallet or exchange app.

---

## Manager

A **Manager** runs a venue (restaurant, bar, café, hotel, etc.) and sets up the team that receives tips.

### What a Manager does

- **Creates the venue** — when you sign up as Manager, your organization is created automatically.
- **Adds waiters** — create a tip profile for each team member (name, optional photo). A QR code is ready immediately, even before the waiter has logged in.
- **Sends invite links** — share a one-time link so the waiter can claim their profile and connect it to their account. Links expire after about 24 hours.
- **Manages the venue brand** — upload the organization logo, set default text shown on tip pages, and help with waiter photos if needed.
- **Prints QR codes** — generate a paper-friendly receipt with logo, QR, and tip address for tables or receipts.
- **Monitors incoming tips** — see pay-in balances for waiters in your venue. Payout addresses and withdrawals belong to the waiter, not the manager.

### What a Manager does *not* do

- Does **not** receive tips into a personal wallet in the app (tips go to waiters).
- Does **not** need a crypto wallet to use the service — sign up with email and a one-time code.
- Does **not** move money on your behalf on-chain.

### Typical flow

1. Sign up → choose **Manager**.
2. Create a waiter profile → QR works right away.
3. Copy the invite link → send it to the waiter (chat, SMS, in person).
4. Waiter opens the link, signs up or logs in → profile is linked.
5. Print or share QR codes for the floor.

## Waiter

A **Waiter** is front-of-house staff who receives tips from customers.

### What a Waiter does

- **Receives tips** — each waiter has a unique tip address encoded in a QR code. Customers scan and pay in USDC.
- **Claims a venue profile** — open the invite link from your manager to connect a pre-created profile to your account.
- **Works at multiple venues** — you can have a **personal** tip wallet plus one wallet per employer. All appear on your dashboard.
- **Checks balance** — see how much USDC you have received (per wallet and total).
- **Withdraws funds** — send your balance to an external wallet address you control (for example a personal crypto wallet).
- **Customizes your page** — upload your photo and, when linked to a venue, optional short text shown above your QR on the tip page.

### What a Waiter does *not* do

- Does **not** need a crypto wallet to **start** receiving tips — the QR and tip address work as soon as the manager creates your profile.
- Does **not** manage other staff or venue settings (that is the Manager's job).

### Typical flow

1. Manager creates your profile and sends an invite link **— or —** you sign up as **Waiter** on your own (personal wallet, no venue yet).
2. Open the invite link → log in or create a waiter account → profile is linked automatically.
3. Share or display your QR so customers can tip.
4. When ready, withdraw USDC to your own wallet.

## Quick comparison

| | Manager | Waiter |
|---|---------|--------|
| **Who** | Venue owner / manager | Front-of-house staff |
| **Sign-up role** | Manager | Waiter |
| **Receives tips** | No | Yes |
| **Creates team profiles** | Yes | No |
| **Invite links** | Sends | Opens / claims |
| **Withdraws USDC** | No | Yes |
| **Crypto wallet required** | No | Only for withdrawal |
| **Multiple venues** | One org per manager account | Personal + one wallet per employer |
