## Overview

**crypto-tips** dey let customers leave tip for **USDC** (digital dollar) when dem scan QR code.

Staff go register for app come choose one of two roles: **Manager** or **Waiter**.

Customers wey dey pay tip **no need account** — dem just scan come pay from their own wallet or exchange app.

---

## Manager

**Manager** dey run venue (restaurant, bar, café, hotel, etc.) and dey setup team wey go collect tips.

### Wetin Manager dey do

- **Create venue** — when you register as Manager, your organization go create automatically.
- **Add waiters** — create tip profile for each team member (name, optional photo). QR code dey ready immediately, even before waiter don log in.
- **Send invite links** — share one-time link make waiter fit claim profile come connect am to their account. Links dey expire after about 24 hours.
- **Manage venue brand** — upload organization logo, set default text for tip pages, and help with waiter photos if e need am.
- **Print QR codes** — generate receipt wey ready for print with logo, QR, and tip address for tables or receipts.
- **Monitor incoming tips** — see pay-in balance for waiters for your venue. Withdrawal address and withdrawals belong to waiter, not manager.

### Wetin Manager *no* dey do

- **No** dey receive tips inside personal wallet for app (tips dey go waiters).
- **No** need crypto wallet to use service — register with email and one-time code.
- **No** dey move money on-chain on your behalf.

### Normal flow

1. Register → choose **Manager**.
2. Create waiter profile → QR go work immediately.
3. Copy invite link → send am to waiter (chat, SMS, face to face).
4. Waiter open link, register or log in → profile go connect.
5. Print or share QR codes for floor.

## Waiter

**Waiter** na front-of-house staff wey dey collect tips from customers.

### Wetin Waiter dey do

- **Collect tips** — each waiter get unique tip address inside QR code. Customers scan come pay with USDC.
- **Claim venue profile** — open invite link from your manager to connect pre-created profile to your account.
- **Work for multiple venues** — you fit get **personal** tip wallet plus one wallet per employer. All go show for your dashboard.
- **Check balance** — see how much USDC you don receive (per wallet and total).
- **Withdraw funds** — send your balance to external wallet address wey you control (for example personal crypto wallet).
- **Customize your page** — upload your photo and, when you don link to venue, optional short text wey go show above your QR for tip page.

### Wetin Waiter *no* dey do

- **No** need crypto wallet to **start** collect tips — QR and tip address dey work once manager create your profile.
- **No** dey manage other staff or venue settings (na Manager work be dat).

### Normal flow

1. Manager create your profile come send invite link **— or —** you register as **Waiter** by yourself (personal wallet, no venue yet).
2. Open invite link → log in or create waiter account → profile go connect automatically.
3. Share or display your QR make customers fit tip.
4. When you ready, withdraw USDC to your own wallet.

## Quick comparison

| | Manager | Waiter |
|---|---------|--------|
| **Who** | Venue owner / manager | Front-of-house staff |
| **Role when you register** | Manager | Waiter |
| **Collect tips** | No | Yes |
| **Create team profiles** | Yes | No |
| **Invite links** | Send | Open / claim |
| **Withdraw USDC** | No | Yes |
| **Crypto wallet needed** | No | Only for withdrawal |
| **Multiple venues** | One org per manager account | Personal + one wallet per employer |
